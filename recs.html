    This is where I will show off all the media I think you should take a look at!
    Though right now it will mainly be albums I think you should listen to!

    Eusexua

    FKA Twigs

    Favorite Song: Childlike Things

    Song that Grew on me: 24hr Dog

    I am surprised I haven't heard of FKA Twigs till recently, because i can't help myself from coming back to this album again and again and again! My friend put me on this on the way to the club, listening to 'Girl feels Good' and ever since then my head has been filled with more twigs than a typical faggot.

    Go ahead and listen to this one if you're a fan of anything electronic and wanna bounce between feeling hit by a truck with emotions and going for a fun night at the club , this is the album for you.

    EDIT: as of today 11/14/25 when i post this, FKA Twigs JUST released a new album, eusexua afterglow, which coinsides with her previous techno-pop album eusexua. will give that a listen.

    JUNGLE WITCH

    vertigoaway

    Fav Song: The Jungle Witch

    Song That Grew on me: The Unhexening

    This may be the epitome of 'jungle' to me. It is stuck in my head mercilessly, and i feel trapped in its atmospheric rhythm -- the witch herself has commanded me like a puppet on a string to 'unce unce unce' my head to the rhythm and close my eyes to imagine its world.

    There is never a bad day or a bad time for these digital vibrations to come to life and seranade my ears, and yet i haven't delved into much of vertigoaway's other projects as of yet, it's as though this one will keep me close and make sure i sing only its praises for all of time. Someone save me.

    The Escape from Blue Marble

    Strxwberrymilk

    Favorite Song: Pluto Raving

    Song that Grew on me: The Moon, As We Know It

    I could've sworn this album is older than 2021, I've beenn playing Pluto Raving as far, as I can remember this post covid lifestyle. Genuinely might be one of the most played electronic song in my library.

    With that being said however, I never listened to much of the album until very recently when I saw the album was name your price in bandcamp. I also had God Hates Strawberries in my playlist, so when i realized those two were in the same album I knew I had to send some bucks my way and listen.

    I ended up falling in love with this album, it's like a subtly sweet drink i know i can always have and fall back into. Seeing the story line from going from Earth to Escaping on Pluto felt so nice. Let's get loose, and enjoy. Don't get in your head, make your music, and and most of all vibe, because you never know how far you're gonna get until you try.

    Shelter

    Alice Phoebe Lou

    Favorite Song: Shine

    Song that Grew on me: Shelter

    Alice Phoebe Lou Means a lot to me ever since listening to Paper Castles back in 2023, and this album always sends me back to a time of Requited love and sensitive losses.

    It stilts me to a place outside of the typical timeline where i can see my past and future selves, and the love of the people around me and the pieces of me I gave away. I can feel the current warmth of my lover's embrace when I listen to Shine, the Pain of the past me when i used to Open My Door to anyone, the wisdom of my future self when i listen to My Girl.

    Highly recommend, and i think you'll find yourself wanting to recommend this to others around you as well.
